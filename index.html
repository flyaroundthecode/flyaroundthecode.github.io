Sasan Gir or The Gir Forest National Park and Wildlife Sanctuary is a shelter to numerous mammal and reptile species. By staying at our Hotel, you gain the opportunity to witness this incredible diversity of wildlife with your own eyes..

Welcome

We recommend you to start with a jeep safari to watch and film the local wild fauna. Lion prides here are used to the presence of people, so you will be able to see them in detail from a close distance.


In addition to the safari, we offer tours to the majestic Somnath temple, the stunning Diu island, the calm and meditative place of Satadhar and other picturesque, ethnic locations. If you want your Gujarat experience to be complete, make sure to take at least one of these excursions.
