About Us





"Travel is the only thing you buy that makes you richer."

We are a part of 30 acre self sufficient Farm. We are more of a jungle theme hotel not a professional hotel. We don't sell rooms! We connect you to nature! We promote adventures and share our wild life experiences with you. We are one of the oldest, eco friendly and valid licensed property in Gir! We don't boast a great luxury. We don't have lush green lawns or manicured gardens, simply because its not a part of true wilderness. We don't have formal uniformed staff or professional help desk or travel desk. We promote adventures and expose you to various wilderness activities.
We are a small, quiet, self sufficient, simple jungle like camp accommodation. We have 6 AC Suites, 4 AC Delux Rooms and 4 Non-AC Rooms. We have a Lovely Elevated House, ten feet above the ground, to give you real jungle feel. with children's play area, adventure sports, jungle rides and photography point that offers you privacy, peace and a great holiday experience too!
We are self-sufficient for our major requirement of dairy products, crops, fruits & vegetables! We grind out own flour for the day! We cook on fire wood! Unlike other hotels we don't have many rules or sign boards around. We also grow ground-nuts, sugarcane, wheat & pulses like grams & mangoes. We grow our own onions, potatoes, tomatoes, garlic, cabbage, cauliflower, brinjal, carrot, radish, beans, beetroot.
We promote Kathiawadi culture and Kathiawadi cuisines but we also serve Punjabi cuisines along with Kathiawadi cuisunes. We are also professional with an exposure of cultures from around more than one hundred countries !!
