Services

Restaurant

Hotel Bhavesh Farm has a wide selection of dining options to choose from, 24/7. Be it regional home-styled food, healthy 'active food', we have something to suit every palate.

Rooms

Equipped with smart, spacious rooms with well-appointed interiors, the hotel offers 24x7 room service with contemporary effficiency.

There is a dedicated play area for children, with special equipment to enjoy swinging, climbing and sliding.

Unlike other Gir Sasan hotels, Hotel Bhavesh Farm is located close to the park, allowing you to marvel at natural landscapes by looking out of the window of your tent.
